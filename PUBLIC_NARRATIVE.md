# CogniTrace Public Narrative

## One-Sentence Positioning

CogniTrace is a proof-of-process layer for human intellectual work in the AI and agent era.

## Problem Statement

In many workflows, people are judged by post-hoc AI detectors on final text only.
That approach can produce false positives and cannot explain how work was created.

## What CogniTrace Adds

- process evidence rather than output-only judgement
- replayable timeline for transparency
- local-first MVP for practical adoption

## Audience Priority

1. Students and writers facing false-positive anxiety
2. Educators and reviewers who need explainable process context
3. Agencies and professional creators needing client-facing proof

## Messaging Guardrails

- Say: "proof of human process"
- Say: "transparency and evidence"
- Do not say: "guaranteed cryptographic truth" unless implemented end-to-end
- Clearly mark beta/mock features
