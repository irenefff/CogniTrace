# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in CogniTrace respectful and inclusive for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and discuss ideas, not people.
- Welcome different backgrounds and communication styles.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, intimidation, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and comments that violate this code.
Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent bans.

## Reporting

Report incidents to: replace-with-real-support@cognitrace.ai

All reports will be reviewed as promptly and fairly as possible.
